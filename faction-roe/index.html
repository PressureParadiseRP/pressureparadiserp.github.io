Pressure in Paradise RP

⚖️ Faction Rules of Engagement

The purpose of these Faction rules is to provide clear guidance and expectations for all whitelisted factions under Pressure in Paradise RP Faction Management. This document covers faction management standards, protocol, and conflict rules you must follow while operating as a faction.

Rules are subject to change. Any updates (and/or rule violations) will be communicated through announcements in the faction Discord. You are expected to bookmark this document and reference it regularly to avoid preventable rule breaks.

Factions that do not comply with these rules (and/or server rules) may be subject to faction strikes, disbandment, member removals, and/or faction perks being revoked. Certain situations may be handled directly by City Government processes and may not involve Faction Management (example: VDM/RDM).

Strike policy: After 5 strikes, a faction will be disbanded and faction property (including hoods/turfs) will be removed and made available for new factions. Faction Management may also disband a faction due to repeated reports of subpar RP. Leaders may submit a ticket to appeal a strike; appeals are reviewed and may be denied.

General Faction Information
  • Factions have a maximum of 35 members.
  • Interactions must be posted within 24 hours. Submissions after 24 hours are invalid.
  • Interactions must include clips showing the beginning, middle, and end of the situation.
  • Any OOC in an interaction report will automatically deem the report invalid.
  • Push-to-talk is mandatory for all faction members.
  • Unfair advantage packs are not allowed (example: footstep audio packs or audio/graphics that provide unfair advantages).
  • Graphics packs containing props, tints, ped lights, timecycle/drug effects, etc. are not allowed.
  • Faction Management approval is required for official interactions or storyline claims.
  • Scale menus are not allowed for faction members.
  • If you are killed during a shootout/attack/defense, you must follow NLR (see PIP rules for details).
  • If you “go in your head,” roleplay does not stop. Find a safe location to continue your RP.
  • If your faction is inactive for 14 days, it will be disbanded.
  • If you leave your faction, get dropped, or your faction is disbanded, you must wait 7 days before joining/creating a new faction.
Character Development Progression

Faction RP should reflect character progression. Your permissions are tied to logged negatives:

  • 3 negatives logged = allowed to use melees.
  • 4 negatives logged = allowed to rob.
    • Rob-on-sight is allowed on blocks.
    • Off-block robberies require 1 minute of RP prior to the robbery attempt.
  • 6 negatives logged = shooting terms.
    • KOS is on blocks only.
    • No shooting off-block unless a robbery goes wrong (valid escalation).
Faction Interactions
  • All interactions must be posted within 24 hours or they will be invalid.
  • Interactions must include clips showing the start, middle, and end of the scenario.
  • Interactions must make sense and connect naturally — no random conflicts.
  • No starting fights in green zones.
  • Provoking or escalating in green zones will result in faction punishment.
  • No third-partying ongoing faction scenes.
  • After a scene, leave the area. Do not camp, lurk, or circle back to force re-engagement.
  • Birdy/social media disputes must be argumentative conversation and you must know who you are arguing with.
  • You must wait 30 minutes before attempting to interact with the same faction you just had a scene with.
Faction Attacks & Cooldowns
  • A faction attack is when you attack another faction with firearms (or other weapons) on the 4th interaction, whether on or off the block, leaving them in death mode.
  • 8-member slide limit block-to-block (max 2 cars).
  • You can either rob or kill — not both in the same scene.
  • No looting or stealing vehicles after shootings.
  • You must have a chain or flag on for all attacks.
  • If you attack a block, there is a 1 hour cooldown before the opposing faction can slide back.
  • No double attacks. You must wait 1 hour to attack that same faction again.
  • Cannot carry or hide in trunks when attacking.
Faction Defense
  • If a car spins your block more than twice, you may shoot only if active beef exists.
  • If a faction comes to your block and you ask them to leave 3 times, they have 1 minute to leave before lethal force is allowed.
  • No rooftop/highway/mountain/balcony/hill shooting. Elevated camping is not valid.
  • Factions that were just attacked have 1 hour to retaliate before the opposing faction can slide again.
Robbery Rules
  • You cannot rob someone on a bike or in a car with the engine on.
  • You cannot rob anyone with a melee.
  • You cannot rob a group larger than 3 (including civilians).
  • You cannot rob someone who has a gun out (they must fear the gun).
  • Chain robberies are not allowed. There is a 1 hour cooldown on all robberies with the opposing faction from the first attempt.
  • 3:1 scenarios only: If you are caught off guard or have guns pointed at your face, you must comply whether you have a gun in hand or not. Failure to comply may result in a CK and FRP ban. Do not avoid RP or powergame “1 minute interaction” attempts.
  • No fist-fight robbery setups: Fist fights and robberies cannot happen in the same scene. Starting a fist fight to make someone put guns away to set up a robbery is not allowed.
Allies
  • Factions can only be allied with one other faction at a time.
  • Allies can attack factions together as long as both factions share the same beef.
  • Allies may be on the same radio once confirmed by Head Faction Management (HFM).
  • Allies must still hold their own block and should not be together 24/7.
  • You need 6 positive innos logged with the allied faction and must open a ticket for FM approval.
  • 2 strikes for violating ally rules will void the alliance agreement.
Probation

New factions are placed on a 7-day probationary period under review for RP quality and faction/character development. During probation they cannot be provoked or attacked unless they provoke other factions first.

CK Rules

How can I be CK’d?

  • FRP robberies: Failing to comply with a robbery and not valuing your life. Any rule breaks in a CK clip can deem it invalid and may be charged as FRP with a pocket wipe.
  • Leaving a faction: Automatic CK unless otherwise approved. Your faction has 12 hours of playtime to locate you for a CK scene. Avoiding RP and not flying is not tolerated — your playtime must reach an additional 12 hours from when the CK is submitted. Management will CK otherwise. You cannot dodge CKs by switching characters or “flying in on another soul.”

Body Bags

  • Body Bag CKs are Faction vs Faction only (not Faction vs Civilian).
  • A 2-minute clip must be logged in your thread.
  • If reported and not completed within 24 hours of the scene, your faction will receive a strike and the CK will be overturned.

If you are CK’d, you may reclaim donation vehicles that you personally purchased (not gifted vehicles). Staff will validate claims — have required information ready (CFX/FiveM name, proof, etc.).

Civilian Interactions
  • Civilians cannot slide with gangs.
  • Civilians cannot be robbed or killed unless they heavily provoke or threaten a gang member.
  • Civilians hanging with gang members on their turf can be targeted by opposing factions.
  • Civilians in active block wars must fear for their lives.
  • Killing or robbing a civilian without prior piggybacked RP can result in a ban.
PD / EMS Interactions
  • All shooting stops when EMS arrives. The scene is over once healed.
  • Respect EMS. Longer crutches may be applied if EMS is disrespected.
  • Fear PD. Do not act fearless when outnumbered or pursued.
  • If PD or EMS arrive, do not move bodies.
  • If you were not part of the original scene, do not jump in mid-fight to save friends from PD. No ambushing PD or non-RP escape attempts.
  • PD can confiscate illegal or donation guns and issue fines.
  • No GTA-style driving.
  • PD units must move together; no solo-cop behavior unless it’s 1v1. PD should fear gangs when solo, and gangs should fear PD at all times.
  • Maintain mutual respect. Both sides must promote immersive and fair storytelling.
Bannable / Strike Offenses
  • Factions receive strikes if 3 or more faction members are breaking a rule in the scene.
  • Faction sliding with a civilian/criminal.
  • No shooting or robbing people on crutches. Players on crutches are helpless — roleplay your injuries.
  • Social media: no remembering or initiating on Twitter, no posting dead bodies, etc. If you want to post without PD knowledge, use the dark web app or a burner account.
  • You must claim your hood anytime you’re asked (unless it’s PD or EMS). Failure to comply may result in a faction strike.
  • No faction interactions or attacks in the last 30 minutes or first 30 minutes of tsunami. Tsunami resets all cooldowns.
  • Shooting from vehicles is only for slider vehicles.
  • False claiming another faction = faction strike.
  • Civilians wearing gang attire or claiming your set can result in a faction strike.
POV Requests / Cheating
  • POV requests are typically for 2 or more kills. However, if FM approves a 1-kill request, you must provide POV.
  • If Faction Management requests POV and you do not provide it within 24 hours, the following applies:
  • 1st No POV: 450
  • 2nd No POV: 600
  • 3rd No POV: Character CK + faction strike
